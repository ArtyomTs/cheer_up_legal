Privacy Policy

Cheer Up · Last updated: 8 July 2026

This policy explains what data the Cheer Up app ("the app", "we") collects and how it is used. By using the app you agree to this policy. Questions: admin@nano-spiel.at.

No personal account

The app does not require you to sign up or provide your name, email, or phone number. On first launch we create an anonymous identifier (via Google Firebase Authentication) so the app can function. It is not linked to your real-world identity.

Information processed

  • Content you enter (recipient name, occasion, optional facts, or your own lyrics and music-style text) is sent to third-party AI service providers to generate the song lyrics and music. Do not enter information you do not want processed for this purpose.
  • Photos and videos you select are processed on your device to compose the greeting video. They are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Diagnostics: if the app crashes, crash reports (device model, OS version, anonymous identifier, technical logs) are collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.
  • Usage safeguards: an anonymous device identifier and request counts are stored (Firebase) to prevent abuse and enforce fair-use limits of the AI features.
  • Configuration: the app fetches non-personal configuration via Firebase Remote Config.

Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by the platform’s app store (Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment or card details. The store provides us only with confirmation of a completed purchase.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not upload your photos, videos, or finished greetings to our servers.

Third parties

We rely on the following processors: Google Firebase (anonymous authentication, crash reporting, remote configuration, abuse prevention), Apple (in-app purchases), and third-party AI service providers used to generate lyrics and music from the text you enter. Each processes data only as needed to provide these features.

Data retention & deletion

Your greetings and Cheer balance are stored locally on your device. You can delete an individual greeting at any time in the app, and removing the app deletes all local data. You can also erase everything at once from Settings → Delete My Data, which removes your greetings and Cheers from the device and deletes the limited server-side records tied to your anonymous identifier. Alternatively, email admin@nano-spiel.at to request deletion.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date above will change accordingly.
