Common questions

What are Cheers? Cheers are used to generate a song. You get 5 for free, one song costs 1 Cheer, and you get +1 back every 24 hours (up to 5).

Why is my video watermarked? The cheaper unlock adds a watermark and a short branded outro. The full unlock removes both.

Restore purchases? Settings → Restore Purchases.

Delete my data? Settings → Delete My Data erases your greetings, Cheers and anonymous account records.
